About

I am a 17 year old girl with the heart of a 1000 year old demon. I keep it in a jar in my room. You dont know me yet but the testimony of my art should tell you more about me than some little blurb. I can paint, draw, sculpt, and bead. I have experience with digital arts and film. Having dabbled in many art forms, I bring to you only the best of my work. I wont give you my life story, novalist is not on my list of skills, SO I will simplify it to a short list comprising all the important facts of my life:
1 I have 2 cats, they are my world and my muses.
2 I love all things cute and fluffy
3 but I also adore the dark and macabe.
4 I am not the sweetest person, but If you like my art, you will probably like me (and I you)
