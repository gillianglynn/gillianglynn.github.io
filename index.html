About Me

I am currently a Market Intelligence Analyst at Grant Thornton. After taking a course in VBA and using it heavily in my role at GT, I was inspired to learn more about coding. In August 2015, I began learning web development through Free Code Camp.

I have a B.S. in Math from Loyola University Chicago and an MBA with Economics concentration from Loyola's Quinlan School of Business.
